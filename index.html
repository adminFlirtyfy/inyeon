Yes — completely free. Download, create your profile, take personality tests, match, and chat without paying anything. No hidden subscriptions, no paywall for messages, no credit system.

Completely free. Once you match, you can chat without limits, without coins, without upgrading. We believe conversation is the most important part of connection — it should never be monetized.
