Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you want to know about Inyeon AI — answered honestly.

Is Inyeon AI free?

Yes — completely free. Download the app, create your profile, take personality tests, match with people, and chat — all without paying anything. No hidden subscription, no paywall to send messages, no credit system. Inyeon AI is a free dating app built on the belief that real connection should never be locked behind a price tag.

Is Inyeon AI an AI dating app? How does the AI work?

Yes. Inyeon AI is an AI-powered dating app that goes beyond swiping on photos. The AI studies your mood, your intent, your personality profile, and how you interact — then matches you with people who genuinely align with who you are, not just what you look like. It acts on what you actually want, not just what you clicked on. The more you use it, the better it understands you.

What are the free psychology tests and how do they help?

Inyeon AI includes free psychology assessments built directly into the app — including the Big Five personality model, love languages, and attachment style tests. These are tools that most people pay for or never access at all. When you complete them, the AI uses your results to understand how you think, how you love, and what kind of connection you are looking for. The tests do not just help the algorithm — they help you understand yourself first. Because the clearest path to finding the right person starts with knowing who you are.

Does the AI truly understand me?

That is exactly what we built it to do. Most dating apps track your behavior — what you swiped, how long you looked at a photo. Inyeon AI goes deeper. It reads your intent: what you are actually looking for, how you communicate, what your personality says about your compatibility. Combined with your psychology test results and mood at the time of matching, the AI builds a picture of you that no simple swipe can capture. The more you engage authentically, the more accurately it matches you.

Who owns my data? What happens to my messages?

You own your data — fully and completely. Inyeon AI stores your chats locally on your device, encrypted, the same way WhatsApp stores your messages. Your conversations never live on our servers. We cannot read them. We do not sell them. No advertiser ever sees them. If you get a new phone or clear your app data, you can securely back up your chats to Google Drive and restore them anytime — just like WhatsApp backup. Your data is yours. We are just the space where connection happens.

What privacy modes does Inyeon AI offer?

Inyeon AI is built privacy-first from the ground up. You can browse and match in Incognito Mode — meaning your profile is not visible to people you have not matched with. You control your read receipts, so you can read messages without the sender knowing. Typing indicators can be turned on or off depending on how much presence you want to show. These are not premium features — they are yours by default, because your comfort and privacy matter more than engagement metrics.

Can I delete or edit messages after sending?

Yes — and this is something no other dating app in the world currently offers together. You can delete any message for everyone in the conversation, just like WhatsApp. You can also edit a message you already sent, so a typo or second thought does not define your conversation forever. Your words, your control.

Is chat with matches really free?

Completely free. Once you match with someone on Inyeon AI, you can chat with them without any limit, without purchasing coins, without upgrading to a premium plan. We believe the conversation is the most important part of connection — it should never be monetised against you.

How long do matches last?

Each match stays active for seven days. This is intentional. Inyeon AI is not designed to keep you endlessly scrolling through an inbox of forgotten conversations. Seven days gives you real time to think, to decide, and to start something meaningful — without pressure. If both people are interested, the conversation will happen within that window. Inyeon is not something you rush.

What is Inyeon? What does the name mean?

Inyeon (인연) is a Korean philosophical concept describing the invisible thread that connects two people — not by accident, but by something deeper. It is the idea that every meaningful relationship carries a bond shaped before the moment you meet. We named the app Inyeon AI because we believe technology should honour that depth, not replace it. We are not building a swiping machine. We are building a space where fate and intention meet.

Is Inyeon AI available on iPhone?

Currently Inyeon AI is available on Android only and is in open testing as of April 2026. iOS support is on the way. If you want to be notified when the iPhone version launches, follow us or check back at inyeonai.com.
